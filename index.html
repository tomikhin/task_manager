About Task Manager

Organize your tasks with our simple and intuitive task management tool.

Here's what you can do:
  • Add new tasks: Stay on track by defining priorities and deadlines.
  • Edit existing tasks: Update descriptions, deadlines, or priorities whenever needed.
  • Mark tasks as completed: Celebrate your progress with just one click!
  • Delete tasks: Keep your task list clean and focused.
  • View all your tasks: Get a clear overview of what’s on your plate.
  • Archive completed tasks: A unique feature that lets you save your completed tasks in separate text files for future reference. Automatic save of completed tasks to remote machine.

Task Manager isn’t just functional — it’s your partner in productivity. Get started now and take control of your day!
